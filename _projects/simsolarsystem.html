--- title: Sim Solar System image: SimSolarSystem.png ext_url: https://github.com/apclemens/SimSolarSystem layout: project content_image: sss.gif --- This was an undergrad project where I simulated the solar system using the Runge-Kutta method. I downloaded the orbital constants from the Jet Propulsion Laboratory for the planets, several moons, and 20 objects in the asteroid belt. I ran the simulation with different timestep values to see the effect of less precision on the stability of the orbits.
